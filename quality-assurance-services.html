Quality Assurance Services

Quality assurance is one of the most critical aspects of software development. Small enterprises typically overlook quality assurance hoping that they are saving cost in the process. The fallout of this fallacy is that they end up burning more dollars as bugs keep piling up and timelines keep on extending.

Quality Assurance is more than testing

Quality assurance is more than testing. It is a holistic approach for ensuring bug-free products, which meet all the functional requirements within the stipulated time frame. After a product is developed and released, quality assurance guarantees that it performs on all environments under all circumstances and no regression bugs are introduced in the subsequent releases.

The role of a quality assurance engineer is more important than a developer. A developer is familiar only with a part of the system but a QA engineer is familiar with the whole system. There is a difference in perspective as well. The developer views the system from a technical perspective but the QA engineer views it from the end user perspective. This end user perspective plays a major role in the success or failure of a product.
